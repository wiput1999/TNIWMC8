Our Mission

For thousands of years, humans have gazed upon the stars twinkling in the night sky and dream of one day getting there. It doesn't have to be a dream anymore, with today's space technology reaching the stars is very possible. With your support, our age long dream shall be realized.

Here, at our Project: Gai HQ, we are working round the clock to build the rocket to reach the Pleiadas. We aim to launch for the stars by 2017. Come join us, to a journey to the outer space.

Help Us!

We can not hope to achieve anything without the support from you. Every baht helps toward making the dream comes true. Anyone who donates will have a chance to join the journey, and everyone who donates will have their name engraved on the stela which will be planted on the Pleiades. 5 people will be randomly selected from the list of our donor to be a part of the crew, that could be you!
